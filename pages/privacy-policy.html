Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At CalcHub ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your information is handled when you use our website at www.calchub.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

CalcHub is designed to operate with minimal data collection. All calculator computations are performed entirely within your browser — we do not collect, transmit, or store any values you enter into our calculators.

We may collect the following non-personal information automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the Site, referring URLs, browser type, operating system, and device type. This data is collected through standard web analytics tools.
  • Cookies and Local Storage: We use cookies and browser local storage to remember your preferences, such as your chosen theme (dark or light mode).
  • Contact Information: If you voluntarily submit a message through our Contact page, we may collect your name, email address, and the content of your message for the sole purpose of responding to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To improve the functionality, performance, and user experience of the Site.
  • To analyze aggregate usage trends and understand how visitors interact with our calculators.
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our Contact page.
  • To serve relevant advertisements through our advertising partners.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device by your web browser. CalcHub uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: To store your theme preference and ensure basic site functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: To collect anonymized usage statistics that help us understand how the Site is used and identify areas for improvement.
  • Advertising Cookies: Our third-party advertising partners, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this Site and other websites on the internet.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.

4. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on the Site. Google AdSense uses cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads based on your visits to this Site and other sites across the internet. This process is known as interest-based or personalized advertising.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your browsing history. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information associated with the Site. Since all calculator operations are performed client-side in your browser, your calculation data never leaves your device. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of any data transmitted to us through the Contact form or collected via analytics.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@calchub.com
  • Contact Page: calchub.com/contact
