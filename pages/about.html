About CalcHub

CalcHub was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to accurate, easy-to-use calculators — without the clutter of ads-first experiences, confusing interfaces, or hidden paywalls. Whether you're a student working through a math problem, a professional planning finances, or someone simply curious about their health metrics, CalcHub is here to help.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make calculations accessible, transparent, and reliable for everyone. We believe that the tools people use to make important decisions — from loan repayments to body mass index — should be free, fast, and accurate. Every calculator on CalcHub is built with real formulas, clearly documented so you understand not just the answer, but how we got there.

Our Story

CalcHub started as a side project by a small team of developers and designers who were frustrated with the state of online calculators. Too many existing tools were slow, bloated with trackers, or produced unreliable results. We set out to build something better — a clean, modern platform that puts the user first.

What began as a handful of financial calculators has grown into a comprehensive toolkit spanning finance, health, mathematics, and everyday utility tools. Every calculator is rigorously tested to ensure accuracy, and our open approach to formulas means you can verify the results yourself.

What We Offer

  • Finance Calculators — EMI, loan interest, investment returns, and more to help you make informed financial decisions.
  • Health Calculators — BMI, calorie counters, and fitness tools to support your wellness journey.
  • Math Tools — Percentage calculators, unit converters, and mathematical utilities for students and professionals alike.
  • Everyday Utilities — Age calculators, password generators, and practical tools you'll use again and again.

All of our calculators run entirely in your browser. We don't store your data, we don't require sign-ups, and we never compromise on privacy. CalcHub is, and always will be, free to use.
