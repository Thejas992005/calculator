Percentage Formulas

1. What is X% of Y?

Result = (X / 100) × Y

Example: What is 25% of 200? → (25 / 100) × 200 = 50

2. X is what % of Y?

Percentage = (X / Y) × 100

Example: 50 is what % of 200? → (50 / 200) × 100 = 25%

3. Percentage Change

% Change = ((New − Old) / |Old|) × 100

Example: Change from 80 to 100 → ((100 − 80) / 80) × 100 = 25% increase
