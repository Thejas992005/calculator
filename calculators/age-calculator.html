How to Calculate Age

Age is calculated by finding the difference between the date of birth and a target date (usually today). The result is expressed in complete years, remaining months, and remaining days.

Age = Target Date − Date of Birth Years = full years elapsed Months = remaining full months after years Days = remaining days after months

Example: If you were born on 15 March 1995 and today is 29 May 2026, your age is 31 years, 2 months, and 14 days. That's a total of 11,398 days or roughly 273,552 hours!

The calculator also accounts for varying month lengths and leap years to give you a precise result.
